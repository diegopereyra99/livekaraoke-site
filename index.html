What it is

The Karaoke You Love.
Reinvented for the Stage.

Born in the student nights of the Cité Universitaire in Paris, our format grew through years of international parties into a citywide phenomenon. Out of this journey came a unique idea: transforming karaoke into a full live-band experience, packed with unstoppable energy and unforgettable moments captured forever!

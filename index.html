What it is

An interactive live‑band karaoke night

Live Karaoke Paris is a high‑energy night out where the audience becomes the show. A professional live band backs you up while you perform the hits you love. The vibe is friendly, supportive, and a little wild—in the best way.

How it works

It’s simple to jump on stage

1) Choose a song

From Beatles & ABBA to Queen, Ed Sheeran, Bruno Mars, Lady Gaga, Billy Joel & more. We love new requests—tell us your song!

2) Join the band

We handle lyrics, cues, and key. You take the mic; we bring the groove.

3) Perform with the crowd

Lights on, friends cheering, a proper stage moment you’ll remember.
